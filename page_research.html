RESEARCH

My research blends Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) and AI research by examining how people experience, interpret, and engage with AI-powered systems in everyday life. Positioned within Human-AI Interaction (HAII), I take a theory-driven approach that spans highly abstract and conceptual work as well as hands-on user studies, system development, and evaluation. Across diverse application domains (e.g., social robots, recommender systems, chatbots, autonomous vehicles), I focus in particular on how trust is formed, negotiated, and shaped through explanations.
