GLENDA HANNIBAL

Thinker & Explorer

I am a computer scientist and philosopher striving to understand human trust in AI and the trustworthiness of AI systems across various domains of application (e.g., social robotics, recommender systems, autonomous vehicles).

Since March 2024, I have been working as a Postdoctoral Researcher in the EXDIGIT project at the Department of Artificial Intelligence and Human Interfaces, University of Salzburg.
