ACADEMIC BIOGRAPHY

Glenda is an independent Postdoctoral Researcher in the EXDIGIT project at the Department of Artificial Intelligence and Human Interfaces, University of Salzburg. She obtained her Doctorate in Computer Science from TU Wien (2022) and holds a BA and MA in Philosophy from Aarhus University.

Her research centers on Human-AI Interaction (HAII) across various domains of application and with a focus on trust and explanations. She also works on expanding methodological reflexivity by stressing the importance of integrating insights from the humanities and social sciences in the development and evaluation of AI.
