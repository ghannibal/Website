BIOGRAPHY

I am currently an independent Postdoctoral Researcher in the EXDIGIT project in the Department of Artificial Intelligence and Human Interfaces at University of Salzburg. I was previously a Postdoc in the Department of Artificial Intelligence at Ulm University and a Predoc in the Department of Visual Computing and Human-Centered Technology at TU Wien.

I obtained my Doctorate in Computer Science from TU Wien (2022) and holds a BA and MA in Philosophy from Aarhus University.
