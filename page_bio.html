BIOGRAPHY

I am currently an independent Postdoctoral Researcher in the EXDIGIT project in the Department of Artificial Intelligence and Human Interfaces at University of Salzburg (2024-2028). I was previously a postdoc in the Department of Artificial Intelligence at Ulm University (2022-2023). I have also been working as a predoc in the Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) group at TU Wien (2018-2022) and in the Department of Sociology at Unviersity of Vienna (2015-2017).

I obtained my Doctorate in Computer Science from TU Wien (2022, with honors) on the topic of interpersonal trust in Human-Robot Interaction. I hold a BA and MA in (analytic) Philosophy from Aarhus University (2015) where both my thesis projects explored and presented the metaphysical dimenssions of social robotics.

In addition to my research and teaching experience, I contribute actively to the international HCI research community, taking on a range of professional responsibilities. Notably, I have served as an Associate Chair for the subcommittee on Critical Computing, Sustainability, and Social Justice at the CHI conference in 2025 and 2026. I am also the Vice Chair of the Austrian ACM SIGCHI Chapter, which is a national research network dedicated to strengthening and coordinating HCI research within Austria.

You can find more information in my academic CV.
