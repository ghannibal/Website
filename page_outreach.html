Blog Posts

Every semester the University of Vienna is posing a question ("semesterfrage"), which they invite members of the faculty to reflect upon and discuss. For the winter semester of 2016 the questions was "how are we going to live in a digital future". I contributed to the discussion by writing a short blog post titled "When an Interface Becomes the Face". I had a lot of fun preparing this blog post not only because I could present some of my work and reflections on social robotics but also because I got the change to present it to the general public. For the summer semester 2017, I reflected on the topic "Gesundheir aus Dem Labor - Was ist Möglich?". I contributed with the blog post titled "Robot Supported Health" where I gave some examples of how robots are currently developed and used to improve the health of people and how the work in laboratories of robot engineers is going to play a significant role in the healthcare of tomorrow.

Photo Competition

During the spring 2017 I participated in the photo competition named “My Research in one Picture” organized by the University of Vienna. The competition aimed to help early stage researchers communicate their research to a general audience and to become more visible. With my picture "Betwixt & Between" I tried to illustrate my work on social robotics from the perspective of sociology. After an initial selection process, my photo was selected by the committee to continue to the second round in which a facebook vote placed it among the top 10 favorites. Although my photo was exhibit during the prize ceremony for the other participants and committee to see, it did not rank among the top 3. However, it was a very good experience to present my work in a more visual format and I still believe that it captures the essence of much of my research back then.
