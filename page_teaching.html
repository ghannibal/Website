Responsible Human-Computer Interaction (HCI)

Institution: University of Salzburg (AT).
Programme: BSc in Artificial Intelligence.
Semester: SS 2026.

This lectures with exercises course explores the theory and methods of responsible HCI and to examine these ideas through real-world examples of designing and using computer-based technology. Responsible HCI rely on a variety of theory and methods to create technology that anticipates and reduces negative social, ethical, and environmental impacts. Students in this course will learn how to apply ethical frameworks, consider diverse perspectives, and reflect critically on technology design to ensure it aligns with broader social and ethical values.
