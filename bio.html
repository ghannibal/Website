BIOGRAPHY

I am currently an independent Postdoctoral Researcher in the EXDIGIT project at the Department of Artificial Intelligence and Human Interfaces, University of Salzburg.

I was previously a Postdoctoral Researcher in the Department of Artificial Intelligence at Ulm University (2022-2023) where I worked on combining my trust research with the topic of Explainable AI (XAI). Part of the TrustRobots Doctoral College (2018-2022) and the Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) group at TU Wien, I obtained my Doctorate in Computer Science (2022) where I focused on the topic of Interpersonal Trust in Human-Robot Interaction. You are welcome to read and download my dissertation, which I passed with distinction. I hold a BA and MA in Philosophy (2015) from Aarhus University where my thesis projects explored and presented the metaphysical dimensions of social robotics and also considered metaphilosophical perspectives.

I contribute actively to the international HCI research community, taking on a range of professional responsibilities. Notably, I am the Vice Chair of the Austrian ACM SIGCHI Chapter, which is a national research network dedicated to strengthening and coordinating HCI research within Austria. Furthermore, I am growing into a leadership role by taking on supervision responsibilities and supporting junior researchers in their development. In addition, I regularly serve as program committee member, associate chair, and reviewer for major conferences and journals. Through these activities, I help maintain quality standards, support colleagues, and contribute to shaping discussions in human-AI interaction research.
